Welcome To Plan Himalaya

Welcome to one of Nepal’s top adventure tour operator – Plan Himalaya . Since our establishment in 2015, we have become trusted name in the trekking world because of our unique loyalty to our customers and our pledge to make your trip to the Himalayas not only an unforgettable and safe adventure, but also one in which we, your guides and Sherpas, will become your friends forever.
