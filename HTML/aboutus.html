About Us

Plan Himalaya was founded in 2015 with the aims of providing most professionalised trekking guide to the customers and it has been most successful and leading trekking agency of Nepal in terms of providing services to its customers. We provide touring and trekking facilities at very efficient costs by which many people can enjoy our services. We have facilities for trekking to many popular Himalayas of Nepal such as Everest base camp, Annapurna Circuit Trek , Chandragiri Hills etc. “Plan Himalaya” provides customer-friendly facilities through its wide Network which are spread all over Nepal. The company has over 200 guides which are very skilled and experienced. Our team consist of very experienced Sherpa guides who are well known for mountaineering.
